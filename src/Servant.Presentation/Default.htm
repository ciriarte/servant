What is Servant for IIS?

Servant is a piece of software that transforms your regular Internet Information Services (IIS) Manager to a modern, fast and web-based website management tool.

It's designed to fit your daily routines, optimized to track down your worst problems and created to stop you from wasting time on administration.

Web interface

Servant is accessed as any other website. You just enter your credentials from a web browser and you instantly get access to every setting on your IIS.

The web interface is the core of Servant. There isn't any application nor server console - everything happens over HTTPS. You'll never need to remote connect to your server or install any additional software on your workstation to manage your server. You just enter the URL of your Servant-enabled server, even from your smartphone.

Simplified tasks, more features...

Getting all the settings right can be hard. Especially when you have to find just the right one between hundreds of unnecessary options. With Servant everything is simplified and automated as much as absolutely possible.


Example of managing a default IIS site.

Other than simplifying existing features, we've also added new ones:
Live stats for your sites, performance monitoring, safe deployment with warm-up, Git publishing, webfarm synchronization with no additional setup, and much more!
